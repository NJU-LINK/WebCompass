Welcome!

What we do

Clifton Oaks, Inc. is a private facility in Ocala, FL, concentrating in the proper education of young hunters and jumpers. We compete in shows along the East Coast, including the Ocala Winter Circuit, HITS Culpeper, and the Vermont Summer Festival. In addition to horses for sale, we offer full service boarding, pasture boarding, foaling service, trailering (long and short distance) and clipping.
